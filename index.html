Popular Indian Recipes

Indian recipes are known to be enriched in spices, which is why it can be a great idea to add a bit of spice in your usual food routine.
So here are my top 3 Indian dishes you must try!:

  • Paneer Tikka Masala
  • Masoor/Red Lentil Dal
  • Tandoori Chiken

I hope you have a good time making them and enjoy the fruit of your labour.
